### The 2min Interval Challenge

**Why 2min is Different from 90sec**:
- **Neuromuscular**: Legs must sustain force 33% longer
- **Metabolic**: Lactate accumulation higher in second minute
- **Mental**: Harder to push when fatigue builds
- **Recovery**: Longer efforts = longer recovery needed

**Key Success Factors for 2min**:
1. **Pacing**: DON'T start too fast - sustainable effort entire 2min
2. **Form**: Maintain good posture in second minute (don't collapse)
3. **Mental**: Break into 2x 60sec blocks, stay controlled
4. **Recovery**: Full 3min between reps (vs 2:30min for 90sec)
5. **Bailout**: If dying by rep 3-4, reduce remaining reps or switch to easy

**First-Time 2min Tips**:
- **Mon (5x 2min)**: Conservative test - find the right effort level
- **Rep 1-2**: Should feel "comfortable-hard" (RPE 7/10)
- **Rep 3-4**: Should feel "hard" but sustainable (RPE 7.5/10)
- **Rep 5**: Can push slightly harder (RPE 8/10) but don't die
- **Between reps**: Focus on full recovery - HR down to 130-140
- **Mental cue**: "Smooth and strong, not fast and dying"

**Wed (6x 2min)**: 
- **IF Mon went well**: Execute with confidence
- **IF Mon was hard**: Reduce to 5x 2min (same as Mon)
- **Key**: Rep 6 should feel similar to Rep 1-2 = good pacing
- **Watch**: Don't let HR drift down - sign of fatigue
- **Bailout**: If rep 4-5 falling apart, stop at 5 reps total
